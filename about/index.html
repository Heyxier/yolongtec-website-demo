ABOUT YOLONG

Professional Power Tools Built to Last

COMPANY PROFILE

Founded in 2006, Yolong has grown from a small tool manufacturer to a global leader in professional power tools. Headquartered in Suzhou Industrial Park, China, we specialize in engineering high-performance, durable, and precise power tools for professional users worldwide.

Our mission is simple: engineer tools that professionals can rely on for the toughest jobs. Every product we create undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets the demanding standards of tradespeople across construction, automotive, woodworking, and metalworking industries.
